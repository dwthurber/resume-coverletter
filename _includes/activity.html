Think like a hiring manager

Evaluating someone else’s resume can help you glean insights that aren’t as obvious when critiquing your own materials. Several MSHE alumni have graciously agreed to provide their cover letters and resumes for jobs in which they have been interested. Take a look at the application materials below alongside the provided job descriptions. If you were the hiring manager, would you invite them for an interview? What would you be curious about? How would your resume and cover letter stack up?
